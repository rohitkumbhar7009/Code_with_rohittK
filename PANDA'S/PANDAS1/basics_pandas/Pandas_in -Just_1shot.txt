
What is Data Manipulation  And Analysis ?

Data Manipulation 
   . Defination: Changing ,organizing , or preparing data to make it useful and easier to understand. 
   . Goal : To clean ,transform, and structure raw data for better usability.
   Example: 
     - organizing a Grocery List : sorting random items into categories like "Fruits" or "Dairy".
     - Fixing Errors in a student Record : Correcting missing or wrong  grades.


Data Analysis 
    .Defination : Extarcting patterns ,trend and insights from the data to solve problem  and make discison.
    .Goal   :  To answer quetions or identify trends using the data.
    Example: 
      - Analyzing Sales trends : Finding the month with the highest revenue 
      -tracking fitness progress: Analyzing daily steps and calories for act
